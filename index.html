Purchase and activate

No token entry happens on this website. Activation is completed inside the Vertex app.

1. Purchase your license

Use the secure checkout to purchase your Vertex license. You will receive your activation token after payment verification.

2. Receive your token

Your token is delivered by email or WhatsApp. It matches the secure format the software expects: a signed token that includes your user data, timestamp, and the Vertex software code.

3. Activate inside Vertex

Open the Vertex software, paste the token into the activation screen, and your account will unlock for full use.
